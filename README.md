# My Favorite Series: All of Us Are Dead

![All of Us Are Dead Poster](https://www.themoviedb.org/t/p/original/pTEFqAjLd5YTsMD6NSUxV6Dq7A6.jpg)

**All of Us Are Dead** is a SouthKorean horror series that combines coming-of-age drama with a zombie apocalypse. It centers on a group of high school students who become trapped during a sudden zombie outbreak and are forced to struggle for survival.

## Why I Love This Series
- Intense, action-packed zombie scenes
- Emotional character development
- Explores themes of survival, friendship, and sacrifice
- Unique Korean perspective on the zombie genre

## My Favorite Characters
- **Nam On-jo** – Strong-willed and brave
- **Lee Cheong-san** – Loyal and selfless
- **Choi Nam-ra** – Mysterious but heroic

## Most Memorable Scene
> One of the most emotional scenes is when Cheong-san chooses to stay behind and confront the zombies, giving his friends a chance to escape. His brave sacrifice is a powerful display of loyalty and selflessness.
